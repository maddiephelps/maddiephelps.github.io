Location

Explore Eden, NC

Located in the heart of downtown Eden, Ray Commons Apartments stand as a testament to history and revival. The building is part of the Leaksville Commercial Historic District, offering easy access to vibrant local businesses and the serene beauty of the Dan River.

Historical Significance of Ray Commons

Formerly the Central Hotel, this building has been a cornerstone of downtown Eden's heritage. A rusted old hotel sign and the remnants of past businesses remind visitors of its historic importance, contributing to the revitalization of this charming community.
