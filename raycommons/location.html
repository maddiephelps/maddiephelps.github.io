History of Eden

Eden, NC, originally formed in 1967 through the merger of three towns, has a rich history as a hub of commerce and industry. The Leaksville area, one of the original towns, was particularly known for its vibrant textile mills and historic architecture.

The Historic Ray Commons

The former Central Hotel, now Ray Commons, is located on Washington Street in the heart of the Leaksville Commercial Historic District. A rusted old hotel sign and remnants of previous businesses serve as reminders of its storied past as a key downtown landmark.
