AP Style Comma Presentation

An in-depth workshop presentation on AP Style commas, demonstrating professional editing skills tailored for journalists, PR specialists, and social media professionals. This creative project was a professional workshop-style presentation on AP Style comma rules tailored for journalists, PR specialists, and social media professionals. It covered common misconceptions, essential vs. nonessential clauses, and the AP Style approach to introductory elements and the Oxford comma. The interactive session included practical examples and a Kahoot quiz to test audience knowledge, making it both educational and engaging. The presentation demonstrated strong public speaking, technical editing skills, and an ability to simplify complex concepts for varied audiences.

Project Plan: The Rockettes' Social Media Strategy

Performed a detailed social media analysis of The Rockettes, highlighting strengths, weaknesses, and strategic recommendations to enhance their online presence across platforms like TikTok and Instagram. This project involved conducting a comprehensive 30-day analysis of The Rockettes' social media platforms, such as TikTok, Instagram, Facebook, and X (formerly Twitter). It identified key strengths, including their successful TikTok strategy and partnerships, while suggesting improvements like refining their hashtag strategy, increasing content diversity, and enhancing customer engagement. Personal recommendations included collaborating with influencers and storytelling campaigns to humanize the brand. This project reflects expertise in social media analysis and the ability to craft actionable strategies for improving digital presence.
